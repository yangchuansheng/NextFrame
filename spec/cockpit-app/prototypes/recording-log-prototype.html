Design Notes — Time Focus

  • Hero number: total video produced (content time, not wall time)
  • 4 columns: Video Time / Wall Time / Time Saved / Speed ratio
  • Time breakdown bar: Capture (blue) / Encode (purple) / Audio Mux (orange)
  • Every entry 右侧主角: video time (orange) + wall time (blue) + speed ratio (green)
  • 每行 mini bar 显示 capture/encode/mux 占比
  • 展开: 6 cell breakdown (video/wall/speed + capture/encode/mux)
  • 数据全来自 perf.jsonl: record_s / overlay_s / total_s / realtime_x
  • Running 状态: 显示已完成百分比 + elapsed time
  • Failed: wall time 显示红色 + 错误原因
