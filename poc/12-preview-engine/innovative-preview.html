NextFrame Preview Observatory

Not a timeline. A field guide to live HTML frames.

This POC uses a self-contained version of the `engine-v2` contract: each frame is rendered directly into DOM, Canvas, and SVG layers. Hover to scrub, browse by semantic scene clusters, compare two times in parallel, and drag new scene components into the strip without leaving the preview surface.
